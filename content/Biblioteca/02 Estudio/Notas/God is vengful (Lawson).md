#Biblioteca/Notas

> The wrath of God further includes His vengeance upon those who break His law and offend His holiness. To those who rebel, God says, “I will also bring upon you a sword which will execute vengeance” (Lev. 26: 25). “Vengeance” (Hebrew naqam) indicates God’s intense anger against His enemies that inflicts punishment for the offense inflicted against Him.
> \- **Steven Lawson**

- [[Lev 26#25]]
- [[Deut 32#35]]
- Disobedience to the divine moral code always demands full retribution by God, which is death ([[Gen 2#17]];[[2 Kings 14#6]] 2 Kings 14: 6; Jer. 31: 30; Ezek. 18: 4, 20).
