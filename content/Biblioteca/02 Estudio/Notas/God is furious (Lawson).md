> Another description of smoldering wrath is the infinite fury of God against sin… The word translated “fury” (Hebrew evrah) means “outpouring, overflow, excess.” This indicates the overflowing rage of God against His sinful people, before the rising tide of His anger was turned away from them.

> \- **Steven Lawson**
