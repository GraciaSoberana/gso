> One of the central problems in analyzing the central section of Revelation, particularly from 6:1 to 19:10, is the determination of whether various sections recapitulate earlier sections (e.g., the plagues unleashed by the seven trumpets and seven bowls have many similarities) or the author intends to present a chronological sequence of eschatological events in a manner consistent with a history-of-salvation perspective.
