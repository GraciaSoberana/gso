#Biblioteca/Notas

---
>Christ’s programme is summarized in the words: ‘I will build my church; and the gates of hell shall not prevail against it’ (Mt. 16:18, AV). In the midst of the eschatological conflict (indexed by the verb katischuō, ‘prevail’), Christ is calling not merely individuals to himself but an ekklēsia, an entire assembly.
> *- Sinclair B. Ferguson, The Holy Spirit: 191.*
