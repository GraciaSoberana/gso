#Biblioteca/Notas

>  The infallibility and divine authority of the Scriptures are due to the fact that they are the word of God; and they are the word of God because they were given by the inspiration of the Holy Ghost. <br >\- **[[Archibald Alexander Hodge]]**


> La infalibilidad y autoridad divina de las Escrituras se deben al hecho de que son la palabra de Dios; y son la palabra de Dios porque fueron dadas por la inspiración del Espíritu Santo.<br >\- **[[Archibald Alexander Hodge]]**
