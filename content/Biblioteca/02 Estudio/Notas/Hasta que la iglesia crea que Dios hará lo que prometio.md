> The fulfillment of prophecy awaits the day when the church will really believe that God will do all that He has promised to do, and when the church will sincerely aim at entire conformity to the revealed will of God. Then, by the agency of imperfect but faithful men, we may expect God to do what He has promised to do.
> <span class="author">Roderick Campbell</span>
> <span class="book">The millennium</span>

> El cumplimiento de la profecía aguarda el día en que la iglesia crea realmente que Dios hará todo lo que ha prometido hacer, y cuando la iglesia aspire sinceramente a la entera conformidad con la voluntad revelada de Dios. Entonces, por medio de hombres imperfectos pero fieles, podremos esperar que Dios haga lo que ha prometido hacer.
> <span class="author">Roderick Campbell</span>
> <span class="book">The millennium</span>
