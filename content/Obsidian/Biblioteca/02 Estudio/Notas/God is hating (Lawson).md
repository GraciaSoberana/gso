#Biblioteca/Notas

> The Hebrew word sane is used synonymously with the abhorrence and indignation of God. It refers to the divine hate He has for the unrighteous... This strong word “hate” (sane) means “to find repugnant, to utterly reject.” Because God is perfectly holy, He must react against and reject all sin. Moreover, He must also abhor “the man of bloodshed” who commits sin. God will eternally “destroy” the sinner as the object of His wrath.
> \- **Steven Lawson**
