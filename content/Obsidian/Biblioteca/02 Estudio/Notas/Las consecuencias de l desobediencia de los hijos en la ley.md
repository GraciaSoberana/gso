> anyone who dishonors his or her father or mother shall be cursed (Deut 27:16); any stubborn, defiant, disobedient son shall be killed (Deut 21:18–21). Accordingly, it is commanded that “everyone shall revere/fear his mother and his father” (Lev 19:3).
