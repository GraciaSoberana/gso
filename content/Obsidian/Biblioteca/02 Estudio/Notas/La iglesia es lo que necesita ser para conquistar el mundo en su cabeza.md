>  No new weapons are needed for the conquest of the world, nor is there any change of dispensations. Says David Brown, “The Church is already all that she needs to be. She is complete in her living and ever-present Head, having ‘all power in heaven and in earth’ at her command, and getting it too at the destined periods, when ‘the time to favor her is come, even the set time’ ” (The Second Advent, p. 342).
