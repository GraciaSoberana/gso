>[!resaltado]

>The joy of heaven is exceeding great, because it is correspondent to the majesty and greatness of God; and proportionable to the love he bears to his people, and to the infinite price of the blood of Christ. Christ is enjoyed in heaven, and God on whom the saints’ love is entirely fixed, and there God most fully communicates himself to them, and immediately.
> *- William Bates*

--- 

> El gozo del cielo es sobremanera grande, porque corresponde a la majestad y grandeza de Dios; y proporcional al amor que tiene a su pueblo, y al precio infinito de la sangre de Cristo. Cristo se disfruta en el cielo, y Dios en quien el amor de los santos está enteramente fijado, y allí Dios se les comunica más plenamente, y de inmediato.<br ><span class="author">William Bates</span>
