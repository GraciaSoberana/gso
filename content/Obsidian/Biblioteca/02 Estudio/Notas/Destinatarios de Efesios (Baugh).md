> The notion that Ephesians is circular is an attractive idea, but the evidence from antiquity does not support it...
> it is probable that “in Ephesus” dropped out of a very early copy of the epistle and was omitted by a few later copyists and then reinserted by correctors
> \- **S. M. Baugh**
